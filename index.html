My goal is to help people experience what the Linux operating system has to offer, with as little frustration as possible.

What is Linux?

Linux is an operating system for your computer. You can do everything with Linux that you can do with your Windows operating system. Whether you primarily use your computer for business, personal or even some gaming, Linux will work for you. Linux is an "open source" operating system, which means, all of the code is open and able to be modified by you, the user.

Why use Linux?

Linux is safe, hackers spend most of their time writing viruses and malware that will reach the largest audience, which is Windows users. Linux is powerful, there is so much you can do with Linux that you can't do with proprietary software like Windows. Linux is free, you can try as many distributions as you want, all at no cost. I do recommend you make donations to support the hard work of those who create the distributions that you use.
